    Awesome YouTubers




    Watch video tutorials from YouTubers that teach you about technology.

    Extra information Please, take a look at badges.md to know what the badges mean. The order of the YouTubers doesn't mean the quality: all of the YouTubers below are awesome. Every YouTuber is in a specific section/subsection, though it may involve other topics that are not the ones according to the section (but the section the YouTuber is in is its main content). Note that this is not a promotional list of any kind. Check also the Discussions section to read and discuss topics about this list and repository.

    To find a specific topic faster, you can press Ctrl + F and type the topic you're searching for. It's recommendable to open the links with the click of the mouse wheel or by right-clicking a link and selecting "Open link in new tab" so you can keep the list in a separate tab.
